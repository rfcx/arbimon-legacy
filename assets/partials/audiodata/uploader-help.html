Uploading audio files

Steps

  1. Enter upload batch information. Make sure you select the correct filename format for the recording time data.
  2. Add files to the upload queue either by drag and drop or clicking the Add file button
  3. Click start to upload the recordings
  4. After each recording is successfully uploaded, is processed and then will be available to use

Limits

  • Recordings longer than 2 minutes will be split on 1 minute files, with the last file being 1 minute and the remainder.
    Example: a recording with a length of 10 minutes and 30 seconds will be splitted in 9 - one minute recordings and 1 - 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  • This uploader support recordings up to 60 minute in length, for processing longer recordings contact us and we will help.
