Uploading audio files

Steps

  1. Enter upload batch information. Make sure you select the correct filename format for the recording-time-data.
  2. Add files to the upload queue either by drag and drop or clicking the Add file button
  3. Click start to upload the recordings
  4. After each recording is successfully uploaded and processed, only then will it be available for use

Limits

  • Recordings longer than 2 minutes will be split into 1 minute files.
    Example: a recording with a length of 10 minutes and 30 seconds will be split into 9-one minute recordings and 1-one minute and thirty seconds.
  • This uploader supports recordings up to 60 minutes in length. Please contact us for processing longer recordings.
